Group tourism

Group tourism is a new type of travel mode in which travel agencies are the main body of group tourism. Travel agencies or intermediary agencies arrange and plan the trip. Team members travel in accordance with the arrangements of travel agencies, pay the travel expenses in advance in a lump sum and enjoy group discounts on some items. Group tourism usually refers to a group of more than 10 people traveling together.

Travel agencies provide routes, tourists choose to buy, and then tourists in the specified time, place, scenic spot, accompanied by a guide, take the means of transportation, stay in the booked hotel, according to the specified routes to complete the food, accommodation, travel, tour, shopping, entertainment and other travel process.

团队旅游

团体旅游是以旅行社为主体的一种新型旅游模式。旅行社或中介机构安排和计划行程。团队成员按照旅行社安排旅行，旅游费用一次性预付，部分项目享受团体优惠。团体旅游通常是指10人以上的团体旅游

旅行社提供路线，游客选择购买，然后游客在规定的时间、地点、景点，在导游的陪同下，乘坐交通工具，入住预定的酒店，按照规定的路线完成吃、住、游、游、购、娱等旅游过程。
